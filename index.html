The Beauty Of The World
Through My Lense

Capture Nuance

There is beauty in focusing upon the subtle nuances within a photograph. An image is the combination of various elements with differing historical significance. Each photograph tells a story. I'm interested in conveying such a story. I'm lucky enough have the profession of travel photograher. While travelling I gain a glimpse into aspects of humanity, history and what is available to us via the senses. There is complexity in each image.
